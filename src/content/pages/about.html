About Us

About Mai's Massage Therapy

Mai's Massage Therapy brings authentic Thai healing traditions to the heart of Whitstable. Founded by Mai, who originates from Surin in Thailand, our practice combines time-honored techniques with modern therapeutic approaches. Since moving to the UK in 2014, Mai has dedicated herself to mastering the art of Thai massage, working professionally for over two years, including a year at Sunshine Thai Massage and Spa before establishing her own practice.

Our mission extends beyond simple relaxation. We strive to make massage therapy an integral part of our clients' health and wellness journey, providing therapeutic treatments that make a meaningful difference through evidence-based techniques and traditional Thai healing practices. Each session begins with a thorough assessment to understand your specific needs, allowing us to create truly personalized treatment plans that address your unique concerns and wellness goals.

The treatment space at Mai's Thai Massage Therapy is designed with your comfort in mind. Our private, tranquil environment provides the perfect setting for your therapeutic experience, with amenities including comfortable seating, private changing space, and clean facilities. We've created a peaceful sanctuary where you can escape the stresses of daily life and focus entirely on your wellbeing.

What truly sets Mai's Thai Massage Therapy apart is our commitment to viewing massage as preventive healthcare rather than an occasional luxury. We focus on addressing underlying issues and supporting long-term wellbeing through dedicated therapeutic approaches. By combining professional expertise with genuine care for our clients, we help you achieve better health outcomes, improved mobility, and enhanced quality of life through the healing power of touch.

The greatest gift of Thai massage is not just the relief it brings in the moment, but the lasting improvement to one's quality of life. Mai Owner
